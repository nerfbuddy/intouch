Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups are eligible for Camp InTouch?

There are two programs available for different age groups: Junior program for ages 7-12, and Senior program for ages 13-18.

Is medical support available on site?

Yes. Certified professional physicians will be available on site. Additionally, the staff and volunteers are all type 1 diabetics with training in diabetic care.

How long are the programs?

Programs are a week long, starting on sunday afternoon and ending on friday morning.

Can I communicate with my child during the program?

Contact with the outside world is minimized for the sake of immersion, but if the situation calls for it, the kid is allowed to contact their parents. If the parent needs to contact the kid, the staff will be available 24/7.
