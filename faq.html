Frequently Asked Questions

Who can attend InTouch Camp?

InTouch Camp is designed for children ages 8-15 diagnosed with type 1 diabetes.

Is medical support available on site?

Yes. Certified medical professionals and trained camp staff are present throughout all camp activities.

What should campers bring?

Campers should bring weather-appropriate clothing, diabetes supplies, a water bottle, and a packed lunch unless otherwise noted.

How do I register?

Visit the Registration page for meeting times, location details, and contact information.
