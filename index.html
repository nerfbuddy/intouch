Welcome to InTouch

InTouch is an outdoor camp created for children with type 1 diabetes. Our mission is to provide a safe, supportive environment where campers build confidence, form friendships, and enjoy nature while learning important self-care skills.

Outdoor Adventures

Campers enjoy hiking, team games, campfire activities, and nature projects guided by trained staff and medical volunteers.

Diabetes Education

Hands-on workshops teach blood glucose monitoring, insulin basics, and healthy snack planning in an age-appropriate and encouraging way.

Community Support

Families and campers connect with others who understand life with type 1 diabetes, helping everyone feel seen and supported.
