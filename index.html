Welcome to InTouch

Camp InTouch exists to empower Diabetic children, teaching them how to live well, and doing so through bold adventures in the wild.

Wilderness programs

Our programs revolve around roughing it in the wild. Campers will be taught the basics of wilderness survival, will participate in long hikes with blood sugar checkpoints, and will be given the option to sleep in a cabin or in a tent.

Diabetes Education

Campers will be taught the basics of blood glucose monitoring and treatment, but will also learn tips and tricks from other type 1 diabetics in an age-appropriate and encouraging way.

Stay InTouch

Families and campers connect with others who understand life with type 1 diabetes, helping everyone feel seen and supported.
